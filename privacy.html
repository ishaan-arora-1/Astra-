Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Astra (“Astra,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with the Astra mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

The short version

Astra is built to work entirely on your device. We do not run accounts, we do not operate servers that store your readings, and we do not sell your data. Your photos are analyzed on your device and are never uploaded to us.

Information stored on your device

  • Profile details you enter — your name, date of birth, and gender — used to generate your readings and horoscope. This is stored locally on your device only.
  • Your saved readings — kept locally so you can revisit them.

You can permanently erase all of this at any time from Your Profile → Delete My Data.

Camera and photos

Palm and face readings use your device’s camera or a photo you select. Image analysis happens on your device using Apple’s on-device Vision technology. Your images are used only to generate a reading and are not transmitted to us or any third party, and are not stored by us.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment card details. Apple’s handling of your purchase is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We receive only the anonymous entitlement status needed to unlock premium features on your device.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect your name for our own use, do not track you across apps or websites, do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and do not build a profile of you on our servers.

Children

Astra is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your rights

Because your data lives on your device, you control it directly: edit it in the App, or delete all of it via Delete My Data. Deleting the App also removes locally stored data.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
