Managing your subscription

To view, change, or cancel Astra Premium, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, or tap “Manage Subscription” inside the app.

Restoring a purchase

Reinstalled the app or got a new phone? Open Your Profile → Restore Purchases to restore Astra Premium.

Deleting your data

All your data lives on your device. To erase it, open Your Profile → Delete My Data, or simply delete the app.
